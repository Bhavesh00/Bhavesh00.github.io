COVID-19 Cases and Deaths in United States Visualization

Created by Bhavesh Manivannan (bm12) for the CS416 Data Visualization Course.

Introduction


On January 21, 2020, the first COVID-19 case was reported in the United States. Since then, there have been about 35 million cases reported along with 613 thousand confirmed deaths as a result of COVID-19.

This visualization highlights the number of cases and deaths in each state since the pandemic began. It also displays the percentages of the population in each state that have/had COVID-19 and the percentages of the states' population who died from it. 

Data collected by New York Times: https://github.com/nytimes/covid-19-data/blob/master/us-states.csv
Tested on Google Chrome.
